Process

From raw HR data
to retained employees

Seven steps. One outcome: knowing before it's too late.

  1. 01

    Dataset

    Raw employee records — demographics, job data, satisfaction scores, and performance metrics.

  2. 02

    Preprocessing

    Missing values handled, categorical data encoded, outliers normalized for model stability.

  3. 03

    Feature Engineering

    34 workforce signals extracted and weighted by their historical correlation with attrition outcomes.

  4. 04

    Model Training

    Gradient-boosted classifier trained, validated, and calibrated on real workforce attrition patterns.

  5. 05

    Prediction

    Each employee receives a calibrated flight-risk probability, updated with every new input.

  6. 06

    Analytics

    Attrition risk visualized by department, tenure band, pay level, and behavioral factors.

  7. 07

    Recommendations

    Ranked, actionable retention interventions generated automatically from each employee's risk profile.
