

AttriSense AI — Employee Attrition Prediction System

## Overview

AttriSense AI is a Machine Learning-powered workforce intelligence platform designed to predict employee attrition before it happens.

The system analyzes employee-related factors such as job satisfaction, overtime, monthly income, work-life balance, tenure, performance, department, and other HR indicators to identify employees who are at risk of leaving the organization.

The goal of the project is to help HR teams and organizations take proactive retention actions instead of reacting after employee resignations occur.

---

## Problem Statement

Employee attrition is one of the biggest challenges faced by organizations.

Unexpected employee departures can result in:

* Increased recruitment costs
* Loss of organizational knowledge
* Reduced productivity
* Team instability
* Higher training expenses

Traditional HR systems often identify attrition risks too late.

AttriSense AI uses Machine Learning and workforce analytics to detect hidden patterns and provide early warnings before employees decide to leave.

* And other workforce-related attributes

* Attrition (Yes / No)
